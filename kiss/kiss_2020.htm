Kiss

 I was Kiss, the sweetest cat ever! I loved Jack and Denise with all my heart. I gave them unending love and devotion every day of my short life.

I lived to play with them everyday,and give them a little Kiss on the lips. That's how I got my name. I miss them so much, and I know they miss me too. Maybe someday we'll be together again.



This is an eternal flame which burns for me forever, as my beloved owners mourn and remember me forever.



Kiss...we had no grave sadly. So, this is a symbolic stone. It is made with all our love.

September 17,2018

Kiss;

I'm crying. Crying for a long time over you. I haven't written because my health has deteriorated and I find it impossible to add to the pain. However, every single day I think of you, mourn you miss you, love you.

Ten years has passed and it scares me. It's so hard to believe. It's shocking! The sadness has not lessened at all. Every day I think of how beautiful it all was when you were here. You were the light of our lives. You lit up our souls, and we have not been the same since.

I often fantasize as to how beautiful it would be to have you here again even for a bit, but then I'd never let you go! You are part of me Kiss, and you always will be. I feel like I'm missing a vital part, and there is no cure for that ache.

I don't know the meaning of why this happened, but I know it has been ten years of intense pain. It NEVER stops! When I think back to all the wonders it amazes me, and it was all in such a short time.

You made our lives bright and gave us joy...a joy that cannot be explained. It was all wonderful, but so brief!

We could go on and on, and write a long book. Instead, we placed a few sentimental graphics here in the hope that we will be together again someday but this time forever!

Mommy and I love you so much!
