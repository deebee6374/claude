ℹ️ About

Finance Tracker — A self-contained personal finance manager.
All data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage.
Use the Export button regularly to back up your data to a JSON file.

Encryption: Login passwords are encrypted using AES-GCM with a key derived from your master password via PBKDF2 (100,000 iterations, SHA-256).
Note: If you clear browser data, your finance data will be lost — export regularly!
